Want to know a little about me?

Chapter One

Born and raised outside DC. I spent the first part of my life playing every sport that was offered. I was extremely active and enjoyed being outside and around people. Before 6th grade, I moved to Austin where I spent three incredibly eye opening years before returning to DC. During my short stint in Austin, I fell in love with barbeque, water sports, and football.

Chapter Two

Back in DC, everything felt strange as life kicked into high gear again. Being used to the east-coast pace made it easy to coast all the way through high school. I found myself graduating and getting ready to move to Philly and become a Quaker. From day one as a quaker my life revloved around Lacrosse. That remains true even to today. I spent 4 wonderful years at UPenn only to realize that what I was building wasn't for me. What would I do? Say goodbye to Philly and hello to Boulder. Since I've lived in Boulder, I have become deeply invested in every part of my life. My journey is just beginning as today is the beginning of the most exciting chapter yet.
